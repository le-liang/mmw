Download the Multimodal-Wireless Dataset

The Multimodal-Wireless dataset is available for download via global and China mainland mirrors. Please choose the source that best suits your location:

  • Global mirror (OneDrive): Download from OneDrive
  • China mainland mirror: Download from Quark
Tips:
  • The Blender_Towns folder provides Blender-format mirrors of all CARLA towns for advanced scenario replay and visualization.
  • The sunny folder contains the dataset collected under clear weather (sunny) conditions.
